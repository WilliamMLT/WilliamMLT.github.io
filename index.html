About Me

Learn a bit more about me

Letian has recently graduated 1st in class from the UC Davis MSBA. During the MSBA, Letian served as the Project Manager and Business Analyst for the Mondavi Center for his practicum project, where he built models in Python to predict high-performing shows and used Excel VBA to automate sales and revenue prediction. Additionally, he also used Python to web scrape job portals to support career development for the MSBA program as a Graduate Student Analyst.

Prior to MSBA, Letian finished his bachelor's degree in Economics from the National University of Singapore in 2018. He has worked as a Junior Data Scientist for Merck, where he reduced global freight transportation planning rules by 94% and built a staffing management optimization app that received 10+ department requests for similar tools.

As a self-starter, Letian is driven and self-motivated. During off-hours at Merck, Letian completed over 250 hours of learning on Data Science. He also took various certifications over the recent years to learn new skills relevant to the field of data analytics.
